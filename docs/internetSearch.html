The Beginning of the Internet Search

Before google, important key companies were Yahoo, Oracle and Apple, which all resided in Silicon Valley.


Yahoo Starts Out

Yahoo would generate billions of dollars in revenue in 2007 alongside google. Yahoo used ads in their browsers in order to generate more revenue. Their company was unaffected by this, as users only multiplied, which caused more computer users in general. Yahoo began launching new features such as mail, in order to compete with their competitors. This caused them to turn their attention from the actual search engine, leading to its decrease in usability. Many searches would lead to information that wasn’t asked for.


The Rise of Google

Google was developed by two young students from Stanford, which would surge in popularity after the downfall of Yahoo. Its search engine produced better results and more specific to the internet entered. Google had more advanced technology and better information retrieval methods, due to its simplified methods. Many venture capitalists had begun to invest in google, but they continued to receive only small amounts of money in comparison. Google valued a user friendly UI that would benefit the user more than it did the company. Google made sure to separate advertisements from search results. Advertisements were on separate parts of the page and it was a good balance between results and ads. They earned billions of dollars in revenue while earning more users. After it had become public, google had earned 3 billion dollars of revenue.
